## Spawn Schedule & Rotation

Events fire every **20 minutes** — at **XX:00, XX:20, and XX:40** — cycling through four zones in order:

> **Shrine → Arkeum → Orc Village → Seal Sanctuary → (repeat)**

The active zone advances at the top of each hour; all three spawns within an hour share the same zone. **Stones** always spawn **one zone ahead** of Chests in the rotation, and are individual drops (1–4 hits before dissolving). **Chests** are shared by the whole party.
