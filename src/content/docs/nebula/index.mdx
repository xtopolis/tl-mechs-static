## Spawn Schedule & Rotation

Events fire every **20 minutes** — at **XX:00, XX:20, and XX:40** — cycling through four zones in order:

> **Shrine → Arkeum → Orc Village → Seal Sanctuary → (repeat)**

The active zone advances at the top of each hour; all three spawns within an hour share the same zone. **Stones** always spawn **one zone ahead** of Chests in the rotation, and are individual drops (1–4 hits before dissolving). **Chests** are shared by the whole party.

:::note[Blue Beam Warning]
A blue beam appears at the spawn location **1 minute before** each event. Use it to confirm the exact spot and arrive before the chest/stone spawns — both are instantly lootable on appearance.
:::

:::tip[No-Repeat Rule]
Within a single hour, a cluster location **cannot repeat**. Once a chest or stone spawns at a given cluster, that spot is locked out for the remaining spawns of that hour. Use this to narrow down where to watch.
:::
