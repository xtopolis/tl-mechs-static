Each floor has a **Free** reward you claim to advance, and an optional **Greed** bonus — you cannot exceed your Orb's capacity for either.

| Stage | Required | Greed | │ | Purifies to... |
|-------|----------|-------|---|----------------|
| Awakening | 30 | +50 | │ | 0 |
| Dawn | 30 | +50 | │ | 150 |
| Dusk | 30 | +50 | │ | 450 |
| Twilight | 50 | +100 | │ | 900 |
| Abyss | 50 | +100 | │ | 1,400 |
