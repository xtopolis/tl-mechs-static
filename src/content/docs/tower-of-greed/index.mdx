Tower of Greed is a 5-stage progression system with two reward tracks per floor:

- **Free** — spend **30 pts** to progress through a floor and claim the free reward
- **Greed** — spend an additional **+50 pts** on any progressed floor to claim a bonus item

Your point pool has a cap that can be upgraded as you accumulate items. Progress is saved automatically.
