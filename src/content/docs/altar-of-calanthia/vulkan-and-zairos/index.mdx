Vulkan (fire) and Zairos (ice) dual boss encounter.

## Roles Overview

<Tabs>
  <TabItem label="TLDR">
  - Match DPS between parties to hit interrupt thresholds at the same time
  - Assign tether pairs before the fight (for Phase 2)
  - Most groups only use one Tank during Phase 2
  - Stay grouped center during phase 2 most of the time
  - Be on the correct side during [Shoulder Punches](/altar-of-calanthia/vulkan-and-zairos/phase-2/shoulder-punches/) to receive your protection buff
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem label="Tank">
:::tip[Tank Tips]
- Fire tank: During [tether](/altar-of-calanthia/vulkan-and-zairos/phase-1/player-tethers/), stay on the top half of the arena to not blast conals at the party
- Ice tank: During [tether](/altar-of-calanthia/vulkan-and-zairos/phase-1/player-tethers/), stay above the center decorative mark to point ice beams straight across
- Ice tank: Before reentering boss area, wait for Zairos to lift his hand to skip getting [ice prison](/altar-of-calanthia/vulkan-and-zairos/phase-1/ice-prison/) casted on you
- In Phase 2, only one tank is needed, tank from dead center until punches start, then block on your side
:::
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem label="DPS">
:::note[DPS Tips]
- Pay attention to blocks when entering or leaving boss zones
- Clear stacks after cleansing [tethers](/altar-of-calanthia/vulkan-and-zairos/phase-1/player-tethers/) in each phase
:::
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem label="Heal">
:::tip[Heal Tips]
- Prioritize flashwaving your party during [tethers](/altar-of-calanthia/vulkan-and-zairos/phase-1/player-tethers/)
- Keep your group topped up as much as possible to protect against [floor eruptions](/altar-of-calanthia/vulkan-and-zairos/phase-1/fire-floor-eruptions/)
:::
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem label="Tether">
:::caution[Tether Tips]
- It helps to have pins for the DPS and healers to line up on
- During Phase 1, one party should line up and the tethered partner meets them
- During Phase 2, after [punches](/altar-of-calanthia/vulkan-and-zairos/phase-2/shoulder-punches/) goes wide, and then turn into your pin to meet so you dont touch anyone but your partner
:::
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

## Phase 1

Both parties engage their respective boss simultaneously. Match DPS so both parties hit interrupt thresholds at the same time.

<Steps>

1. 100%

   <div>
   Normal rotation — solve Fire and Ice mechs as they appear. The pattern is usually 2 auto attacks followed by 2 special attacks. Mechs are mostly "avoid this by stepping aside, or block."

   Ice tank need to be careful not to get frozen.
   </div>

2. 95%

   <div>
   **[Tether](/altar-of-calanthia/vulkan-and-zairos/phase-1/player-tethers/) interrupt** — match tether pairs across parties.

   You can wait outside for bosses to block before going back in to reduce risk of freezing/death.
   </div>

3. 70%

   <div>
   **[Tether](/altar-of-calanthia/vulkan-and-zairos/phase-1/player-tethers/) interrupt** — match tether pairs across parties.
   </div>

4. 50%

   <div>
   **[Zairos Shield](/altar-of-calanthia/vulkan-and-zairos/phase-1/ice-shield/) interrupt** — break the shield to continue.

   Many groups cleanse stacks after shield breaks and wait for blocks (around 40%)

   Some groups also cleanse stacks around 30% to make sure boss healths and mechs line up going towards 20% interrupt.
   </div>

5. 20%

   <div>
   **[Tether](/altar-of-calanthia/vulkan-and-zairos/phase-1/player-tethers/) interrupt** — Final tether of Phase 1

   The non main tank should be careful to watch cooldowns as their boss dies so they can switch to DPS quickly after.
   </div>

</Steps>

## Phase 2 - Vulkan
_This assume decent T3/3.5 gear_

Tank faces boss North, away from entrance.

DPS and Healers start stacked _directly_ behind the boss.

<Steps>

1. 100%

   <div>
   DPS stands in "dead zone" directly behind the boss to avoid gaining stacks, and easily solve [Ice Prisons](/altar-of-calanthia/vulkan-and-zairos/phase-1/ice-prison/). Only move when necessary for mechs.

   Use Guardians, Artifacts, Remedies at start to avoid mechs first 50% mechs.

   **Normal Rotation**
   - [Ice prisons](/altar-of-calanthia/vulkan-and-zairos/phase-1/ice-prison/)
   - [Dodge roll](/altar-of-calanthia/vulkan-and-zairos/phase-2/jump/)
   - [Shoulder punches](/altar-of-calanthia/vulkan-and-zairos/phase-2/shoulder-punches/)
   - Protection buff mech
   - [Elemental wave](/altar-of-calanthia/vulkan-and-zairos/phase-2/wave/)
   - [Meteors](/altar-of-calanthia/vulkan-and-zairos/phase-1/fire-meteors/) (6 furthest players from boss)
   - [Dodge roll](/altar-of-calanthia/vulkan-and-zairos/phase-2/jump/)
   - [Elemental pose](/altar-of-calanthia/vulkan-and-zairos/phase-2/elemental-pose/) dps check

   </div>

2. 0%

   <div>

   The vanguard DPS races to 0 hoping to kill it before [Elemental Crystals](/altar-of-calanthia/vulkan-and-zairos/phase-2/elemental-crystals/) appear, or to have it low enough that when they appear (Damage Reduction) you can kill the boss before the [Elemental Wave](/altar-of-calanthia/vulkan-and-zairos/phase-2/wave/) wipes everyone (no Damage Reduction briefly).

   </div>

</Steps>

## Phase 2 - Legacy Rotation (T2)

Tank faces boss North away from entrance. Parties stand diagonal to each other on fire/ice arena edges.

<Steps>

1. 100%

   <div>
   Stacks start accumulating immediately

   Boss does autos and special attacks.
   </div>

2. 95%

   <div>
   Elemental orbs spawn, but can mostly be ignored unless DPS is very low.

   **Opener**
   - [Dodge roll](/altar-of-calanthia/vulkan-and-zairos/phase-2/jump/)

   **Normal Rotation**
   - [Ice prisons](/altar-of-calanthia/vulkan-and-zairos/phase-1/ice-prison/)
   - [Dodge roll](/altar-of-calanthia/vulkan-and-zairos/phase-2/jump/)
   - [Shoulder punches](/altar-of-calanthia/vulkan-and-zairos/phase-2/shoulder-punches/)
   - Protection buff mech
   - [Elemental wave](/altar-of-calanthia/vulkan-and-zairos/phase-2/wave/)
   - [Meteors](/altar-of-calanthia/vulkan-and-zairos/phase-1/fire-meteors/) (6 furthest players from boss)
   - [Dodge roll](/altar-of-calanthia/vulkan-and-zairos/phase-2/jump/)
   - [Elemental pose](/altar-of-calanthia/vulkan-and-zairos/phase-2/elemental-pose/) dps check

   </div>

3. Under 50% - Elemental Crystals

   <div>
   [Elemental crystals](/altar-of-calanthia/vulkan-and-zairos/phase-2/elemental-crystals/) spawn (roughly 3 on one half North/South, 2 on the other)

   _Use the same [elemental tether](/altar-of-calanthia/vulkan-and-zairos/phase-2/elemental-tether/) as the symbol above them when DPSing or you get stunned_

   Dodge rolls

   Tank Rotation
   - Auto attacks and specials
   - Turning the boss to give [elemental tether](/altar-of-calanthia/vulkan-and-zairos/phase-2/elemental-tether/) to team to break crystals
   </div>

   Elemental wave

   **Normal Rotation**

4. 30% - Elemental Orbs

   <div>
   Kill [elemental orbs](/altar-of-calanthia/vulkan-and-zairos/phase-2/elemental-orbs/) using tether matching symbol above, or dps race to win

   **Rotation**
   - [Dodge roll](/altar-of-calanthia/vulkan-and-zairos/phase-2/jump/)
   - Auto attacks
   - Special moves

   </div>

</Steps>
</details>
