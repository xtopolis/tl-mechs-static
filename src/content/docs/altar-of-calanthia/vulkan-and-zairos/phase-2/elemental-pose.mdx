**When**
- At the end of each normal rotation cycle, after Meteors and the second Dodge roll

**What**
- The boss strikes a pose, gaining a shield
- The boss becomes resistant to damage from players tethered to the same element it's channeling

**Solution**
- Move to the opposite elemental side to damage through the shield in time

**Penalty**
- Raid wipe
