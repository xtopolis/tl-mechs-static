import { Image } from 'astro:assets';

**When**
- When standing on either side of the boss

**What**
- A tether connects you to the boss in the same elemental side that is towards you

**Solution**
- Move to the opposite side to clear stacks or change tether element

**Penalty**
- Stacks are gained while tethered
