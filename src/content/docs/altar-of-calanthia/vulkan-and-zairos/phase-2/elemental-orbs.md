**When**
- Around 30% (T2 only — typically skipped at T3/3.5 DPS)

**What**
- Elemental orbs spawn around the arena

**Solution**
- Kill each orb using the elemental tether that matches the symbol above it
- Alternatively, DPS race to kill the boss before dealing with them

**Penalty**
- Orbs left alive will continue to threaten the raid
