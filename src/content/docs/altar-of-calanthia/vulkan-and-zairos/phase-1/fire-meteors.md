**When**
- Phase 1: Under 50% after (TBD)
- Phase 2: After the wave passes by

**What**
- Players are marked with a fire icon above their head
- Shortly after, 4 fireballs in a row are dropped on the player, leaving a fire puddle for a short time

**Solution**
- Kite away as the meteor falls and it shouldn't hit you

**Penalty**
- Damage from meteor hitting you
- Damage from standing in puddle
