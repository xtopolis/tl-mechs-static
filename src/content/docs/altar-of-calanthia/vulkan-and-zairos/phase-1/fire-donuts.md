sidebar:
  order: 2
---


**When**
- During boss' normal rotation

**What**
- The boss will telegraph donuts in one of two patterns
- First pulse explodes in the telegraphed area
- Second pulse immediately follows in the previously safe area

**Solution**
- Step outside of the marked area before it explodes

**Penalty**
- Damage taken
