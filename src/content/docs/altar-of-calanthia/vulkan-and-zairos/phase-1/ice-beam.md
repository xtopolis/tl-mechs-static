sidebar:

---


**When**
- During boss' normal rotation

**What**
- The boss targets the tanks location and casts an ice beam

**Solution**
- Avoid it just before it casts, or block

**Penalty**
- You will gain stacks or be frozen for 20 or 40 seconds depending on your stacks
