## Roles Overview

<Tabs>
  <TabItem label="TLDR">
  - Phase 1 interrupts: 90% Orbs, 75% Mirrors, 35% Mirrors
  - Phase 2 interrupts: 90% Orbs, 60% Hand, 20% Wings
  - Assign orb teams, zombie killers, and [bomber teams](/altar-of-calanthia/calanthia/phase-2/detonators/) before the fight
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem label="Tank">
:::tip[Tank Tips]
- Stay within range (~20m) of the boss at all times — she casts chokes if you're too far (exceptions: orbs, mirrors)
- Get back to the boss before the last [Mirror](/altar-of-calanthia/calanthia/phase-1/mirrors/) laser finishes
- 55-60k HP and a great healer can let you survive choke (roughly 65k damage: 12k, 12k, 40k ish hits)
:::
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem label="DPS">
:::note[DPS Tips]
- Kill pusher zombies right away during orbs
- Don't kill Witch's Grasps if they're not in your way
- Have enough HP or mitigation to survive [bomber mech](/altar-of-calanthia/calanthia/phase-2/detonators/)
:::
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem label="Heal">
:::tip[Heal Tips]
- DPS have high DoT stacks coming out of orbs, keep them topped up
- Lightning can one-hit most DPS if they fail to block without mitigation
- [Tethers](/altar-of-calanthia/calanthia/phase-1/tethers/) can shred players with low SDR, in Phase 2 it's even worse as players start stacked on top of each other
- In Phase 2, [patrollers](/altar-of-calanthia/calanthia/phase-2/patrollers/) will spike damage after Fear / [Crystals](/altar-of-calanthia/calanthia/phase-2/crystals/), so try to flashwave beforehand
- [Bombers](/altar-of-calanthia/calanthia/phase-2/detonators/) will also kill squishies, so try to use Shields/Mitigation just before Blast
:::
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem label="Jobs">
:::note[Job Assignments]
**Phase 1**
- Orb teams: 2 anchors, 1 flex per party

**Phase 2**
- Zombie killers: 1-2 players
- Detonator teams: 4 players (each side)
:::
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

   - [Piano](/altar-of-calanthia/calanthia/phase-2/piano/) without knockup

   You have 3 enrage rotations before the arena is covered in purple sludge and you wipe.
