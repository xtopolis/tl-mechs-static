## Roles Overview

<Tabs>
  <TabItem label="TLDR">
  - Play close to boss and other players maximize DPS uptime (ex: during fear)
  - Do not overlap [red/blue circles](/altar-of-calanthia/dragaryle/blue-red-circles/)
  - Assign [baiters](/altar-of-calanthia/dragaryle/clone-baiting/) and [tower](/altar-of-calanthia/dragaryle/towers/) solver jobs
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem label="Tank">
:::tip[Tank Tips]
 - Face boss north consistently
 - Bait [green lines](/altar-of-calanthia/dragaryle/green-lines/) away from pillar locations
 - Maintain 2 lines between each [eye mech](/altar-of-calanthia/dragaryle/eye-mech/) (can fit 3 but 2 is safer)
 - Switch positions after baiting lines
:::

:::tip[Tank Tip]
During the [tower phase](/altar-of-calanthia/dragaryle/towers/), don't move for the first charge and swipes as he'll be charging against back to you. After the boss charges the second time, take two steps back (North)  towards the empty tower spot for the final swipes and pounce. Then you can slowly run towards the tower and place the [green lines](/altar-of-calanthia/dragaryle/green-lines/) and then head back to center as he jumps. This maximizes the uptime for ranged DPS to hit the boss.
:::
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem label="DPS">
:::note[DPS Tips]
- Play close during fear to maximize DPS uptime — leave room for melee if needed
- Be sure to hit blocks and dodges to avoid being caged
- Do not overlap opposite color circles
:::
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem label="Heal">
:::tip[Heal Tips]
- Keep HoTs on the tank
- Flashwave or shield DPS going into fear phase in case they miss blocks
- Flashwave or shield DPS going into tower phase (sub 50%)
- Flashwave or shield DPS during tower solving phase
:::
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem label="Baiter">
:::caution[Baiter Tips]
- You just need to be further back than the other players — you don't need to stand far away
- If towers are up, bait by the arena wall; if there are no towers, touch the boss (after you get marked for baiting)
:::
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem label="Towers">
:::note[Tower Tips]
- Once you start interacting with a tower, don't stop
- Look around for blue orbs as the final fear jump is happening so you can plan ahead
:::
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

## The Fight

<Steps>

1. 100% - 90%

   <div>
   **Opener**
   - 3 swipes
   - [Green line](/altar-of-calanthia/dragaryle/green-lines/)

   **Normal Rotation**
   - 4 swipes
   - [Green line](/altar-of-calanthia/dragaryle/green-lines/)
   - [Clone baiters](/altar-of-calanthia/dragaryle/clone-baiting/)
   - [Eye mech](/altar-of-calanthia/dragaryle/eye-mech/)

   Hitting 90% moves you into next phase
   </div>

2. 90% - 50%

   <div>
   **Fear Phase**
   - 2 Jumps (blocks)
   - 1 Jump back to center (dodge roll)
   - [Red/Blue colors](/altar-of-calanthia/dragaryle/blue-red-circles/) assigned to players, [towers](/altar-of-calanthia/dragaryle/towers/) spawn

   **Tower Boss Skill Rotation**
   - (players solve [towers](/altar-of-calanthia/dragaryle/towers/) during this phase)
   - Tank Rotation
      - 2 Swipes → Charge (blockable)
      - 2 Swipes → Charge (blockable)
      - 2 Swipes → Pounce (blockable)
      - 2 [Green lines](/altar-of-calanthia/dragaryle/green-lines/)
   - Boss jumps back to center (dodge roll)

   **[Rancorwood](/altar-of-calanthia/dragaryle/rancorwood/)**
   - 3 Rancorwood circles (dodge/avoid)
   - During/at the end of this phase, players solve (Block) towers
   - [Clone baiting](/altar-of-calanthia/dragaryle/clone-baiting/) happens if you don't solve towers quickly enough

   **Post Towers Rotation**
   - 4 swipes
   - 2 [Green lines](/altar-of-calanthia/dragaryle/green-lines/)
   - 4 swipes
   - 2 Green lines
   - [Clone baiters](/altar-of-calanthia/dragaryle/clone-baiting/)
   - [Eye mech](/altar-of-calanthia/dragaryle/eye-mech/)

   Hitting 50% moves you into next phase
   </div>

3. 50% - 0%

   <div>
   **[Shield](/altar-of-calanthia/dragaryle/shield/) Break** (interrupt at 50%)

   **Enhanced Rotation** (repeats)
   - Opener: spawns yellow orbs
   - Jump into air → delayed roll dodge
   - Fear phase with Lightning puddles targeting players
   - [Towers](/altar-of-calanthia/dragaryle/towers/) phase with [Blue and Yellow unlock orbs](/altar-of-calanthia/dragaryle/yellow-orbs-blue-orbs/)
   - Enhanced Tank Rotation during Towers
      - 3 Swipes → Charge
      - 3 Swipes → Charge
      - 3 Swipes → Pounce
      - 3 Swipes → Final Slam (avoid if possible)
   - Final slam
   - [Rancorwood](/altar-of-calanthia/dragaryle/rancorwood/)
   - Solve Towers by block/dodge rolls
   - Purple Shield
   
   **Final Rotation**
   - 4 Swipes
   - Jump into air → delayed roll dodge  (yellow and blue orbs, lightning puddles)
   - 4 Swipes
   - Jump into air → delayed roll dodge  (yellow and blue orbs, lightning puddles)
   - Eye mech into dodge roll (if he jumps back, he does two random blocks back to back)
   </div>

</Steps>
