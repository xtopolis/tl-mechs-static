# Belmore

**When**
- Spawns at a random location when Twisted Red Eye (red circle) is applied to a player

**What**
- Belmore chases the player with the Red Eye, attempting to reclaim it

**Solution**
- Run around the arena until all hidden marks are claimed
- Avoid letting Belmore touch you

**Penalty**
- Belmore explodes if it touches the player, usually wiping the group

> [!TIP]
> **Tip**
> You have increased movement speed while carrying the Red Eye
