# Purple Circles

{{< youtube UWRHKmTakjI >}}

**When**
- During the hidden tile mech
- Every 5 seconds the curse activates

**What**
- Curse of Anathema is applied to all players except the Red Eye during the mechanic

**Solution**
- Mark is removed when touching a hidden mark tile, or when all hidden mark tiles are found

**Penalty**
- Standing on a tile during curse activation will cause it to become a Fractured Tile
- Standing on a fractured tile during curse activation will cause it to become a Cursed Purple Tile
- Standing on the same tile as another player during curse activation will instantly create a Cursed Purple Tile
