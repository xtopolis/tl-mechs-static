# Conal Attacks

**When**
- Throughout the fight

**What**
- Nerzatum performs a conal attack in a telegraphed area

**Solution**
- Step out of the telegraphed cone of attack

**Penalty**
- **Suck**: Pulls player into boss, minimal damage
- **Blast**: 2 second stun, minimal damage
