# Shoulder Punches

**When**
- After ice prisons

**What**
- The boss shows block indicators by each of its shoulders
- The boss punches the floor 4 times, alternating sides each time

**Solution**
- Block at least one of the punches on your assigned side

**Penalty**
- Players who do not block do not get a debuff to cleanse, causing you and another to die during wave mech
