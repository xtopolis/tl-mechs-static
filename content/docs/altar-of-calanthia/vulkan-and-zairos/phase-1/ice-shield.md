# (Ice) Shield

**When**
- At 50% health

**What**
- Zairos gains a white damage shield (like Dragaryle)

**Solution**
- Destroy the shield before the time runs out

**Penalty**
- Raid wiped

> [!TIP]
> **Tip**
> Shield cut skills are very effective here
