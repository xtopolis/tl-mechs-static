# (Fire) Jericho Missiles

**When**
- After the tether mech

**What**
- Vulkan shoots several volleys of fireballs at all the players

**Solution**
- Use heals, defensives to keep yourself alive

**Penalty**
- Damage taken is increased the higher your stacks are
