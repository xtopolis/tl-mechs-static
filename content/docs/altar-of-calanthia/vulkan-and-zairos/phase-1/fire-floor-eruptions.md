# (Fire) Floor Eruptions

**When**
- During boss' normal rotation

**What**
- Small fire circles are telegraphed on the floor targeted at players near the boss
- Shortly after, the floor erupts several times out of the marked circles

**Solution**
- Stay out of the circles

**Penalty**
- Decent fire damage, and air knockup
